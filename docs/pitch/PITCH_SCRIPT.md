# DiskiTrack — 2-Minute Verbal Pitch Script

> Rehearse out loud until it flows naturally. Times are approximate.
> Replace every `[bracket]` with your real figure or fact first.
> Total target: ~2 minutes (roughly 300 words spoken at a calm pace).

---

## [0:00–0:20] Hook + problem
"Thousands of football clubs and academies in South Africa are flying blind.
They run on WhatsApp groups, paper team sheets, and spreadsheets. They cannot
measure how their players are developing, and the professional tools that could
help — Hudl, Wyscout, Veo — are built and priced for elite clubs, far out of reach."

## [0:20–0:45] Solution
"DiskiTrack fixes that. It is one affordable, mobile-first platform where a club
tracks matches live from the touchline, sees player and team performance in clean
charts, and manages squads, training, and standings — across every age group, from
under-13 to seniors and ladies teams. Think of it as the operating system for a
football club."

## [0:45–1:05] Proof / traction
"And this is not a concept. The product is already built and live in production.
It is a secure, multi-tenant platform — every club's data is fully isolated, with
role-based access for admins, coaches, and analysts. We are in the final development
stages, and our next milestone is signing our first paying pilot clubs.
[Mention any real interest: "We already have [X] clubs on a waitlist."]"

## [1:05–1:35] Business model + economics
"We make money through a simple SaaS subscription — billed per club, per month,
in tiers. Our blended price is about [R450] per club per month, at roughly 95
percent gross margin, because the platform is multi-tenant: serving one extra club
costs us almost nothing. That gives us strong unit economics — an LTV to CAC ratio
of around [11 to 1] and a payback period of about [two months]. Our fixed running
costs are only about [R1,500] a month, so we break even on infrastructure at just
four paying clubs."

## [1:35–1:55] Market + vision
"There are [X] target clubs and academies in our beachhead market, and we expand
from one club to its age groups, then to partner clubs and leagues, and ultimately
across Africa — with add-on revenue from video analysis, scouting, and payments."

## [1:55–2:10] The ask
"We are raising [R amount] in exchange for [equity % or terms]. That funds our sales
push and final polish, and takes us to [milestone — for example, 100 paying clubs and
R50,000 in monthly recurring revenue] within [timeframe]. I would love to walk you
through the numbers."

---

## Delivery checklist
- [ ] Know your **three numbers** cold: price (~R450), market size, the ask.
- [ ] Open with the problem, not the technology.
- [ ] Say "built and live" early — it de-risks you instantly.
- [ ] Never claim "no competitors"; position as affordable and grassroots-focused.
- [ ] End on the ask, then **stop talking** and let them respond.
- [ ] Have the one-pager and financial model ready to hand over.
