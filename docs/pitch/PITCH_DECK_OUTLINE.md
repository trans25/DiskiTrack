### Slide 1 — Title
- **DiskiTrack** + logo
- Tagline: *"The operating system for football clubs."*
- Your name, contact, date.

### Slide 2 — Problem
- Grassroots clubs run on WhatsApp, paper, and spreadsheets.
- Player development is not measured.
- Pro tools are too expensive and complex.
- *One striking line: "Thousands of clubs are flying blind."*

### Slide 3 — Solution
- Live match tracking + player analytics + squad/training management.
- One platform, mobile-first, affordable.
- Show 2–3 real screenshots (landing page, dashboard, live match).

### Slide 4 — Product / Demo
- Screenshots or a short live demo of the real, deployed app.
- *Emphasise: this is built and live, not a concept.*

### Slide 5 — Market

### Slide 7 — Traction / status
- Built and deployed (Render + Vercel), final development stages.

### Slide 10 — The ask
- Amount: **R[amount]** for **[equity % / terms]**.

---

## Delivery tips
- Lead with the problem and a real screenshot; finance people trust what they can see.
- Know your **three numbers** cold: price (R450), market size, and the ask.
- Never say "we have no competitors" — it signals no market.
- Be honest about traction; a credible plan beats inflated claims.
- End on the ask, then stop talking and let them respond.
