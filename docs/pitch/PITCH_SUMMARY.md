# DiskiTrack — Investor Pitch Summary

> A multi-tenant SaaS platform that gives football clubs live match tracking,
> player analytics, and squad management from one phone, tablet, or laptop.

---

## 1. The one-liner
**DiskiTrack is the operating system for grassroots and academy football clubs** —
match tracking, player stats, training, and standings in one affordable, mobile-first platform.

## 2. The problem
- Grassroots clubs and academies run on WhatsApp groups, paper, and spreadsheets.
- Player development is not measured, so coaching decisions are based on memory, not data.
- Professional tools (Hudl, Wyscout, Veo) are priced for elite clubs and are too expensive
  and complex for the thousands of community clubs below them.

## 3. The solution
A single platform where a club can:
- Track a match live from the touchline (phone/tablet) and capture every key event.
- See player and team performance in clean charts.
- Manage squads, line-ups, training attendance, and league standings.
- Run multiple teams (under-13, under-17, ladies, seniors) under one club account.

## 4. Why now
- Smartphone penetration means every coach already carries the hardware.
- Cloud hosting makes the cost-to-serve per club almost zero.
- Demand for measurable player development is growing across African football.

## 5. Business model
- **SaaS subscription**, billed per club per month, in tiers based on number of teams/players.
- High gross margin: one platform serves unlimited clubs at near-zero marginal cost
  (multi-tenant architecture).
- Expansion revenue: video analysis, scouting/recruitment, payments, and other sports.

## 6. Market
- **TAM:** every football club, academy, and school football programme in target regions.
- **Beachhead:** South African grassroots clubs and academies, then the rest of Africa.
- Land with one club, expand to its age groups and partner clubs / leagues.

## 7. Traction / status
- Product is built and deployed (live on Render + Vercel) and in final development stages.
- Multi-tenant, role-based, secure foundation already in place.
- Next milestone: first paying pilot clubs.

## 8. Competition & moat
- Competitors (Hudl, Wyscout, Veo) target the top of the pyramid at premium prices.
- DiskiTrack wins on **price, simplicity, local focus, and mobile-first design**.
- Moat grows with club data, league/federation relationships, and switching costs.

## 9. The ask
- A defined amount of funding to take the product from final development to first revenue.
- Use of funds: customer acquisition, sales, polish, and support.
